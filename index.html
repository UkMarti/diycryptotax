Questions people ask

Do I need to be a crypto expert to use this? ⌄
Not at all. If you know your wallet address (the long code starting with bc1q or 0x), that’s all you need. We do everything else.
Is my information safe? ⌄
Yes. We only use your public wallet address — the same one you’d give to someone to send you crypto. We never ask for your password, private key, or seed phrase. We never store your data after generating your report.
What if I have multiple wallets? ⌄
Run the tool once for each wallet address. Each report is £10/$10. If you have lots of wallets, you might want to talk to an accountant — our sister site TaxChain Crypto is built for that.
Does this work for Bitcoin and Ethereum? ⌄
Yes, both. We also support most ERC-20 tokens on Ethereum (like USDC, LINK, UNI etc). If you have Solana or other chains, we’re working on adding those soon.
I only made a small amount — do I still need to report it? ⌄
In the UK, you only pay tax if your total gains are over £3,000 in a tax year. In the US it depends on your income. But HMRC still wants to know about it even if you don’t owe anything — so it’s worth checking. Our report tells you clearly whether you owe anything.
Can I send the report to my accountant? ⌄
Absolutely — that’s exactly what most people do. Just forward the email we send you, or print a PDF copy. The report is laid out clearly so any accountant can read it easily.
